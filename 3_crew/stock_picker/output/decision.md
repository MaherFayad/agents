### **Investment Decision Report: Saudi Travel & Aviation Sector**

**Selected Company:** **Seera Group (Tadawul: SEERA)**

**Why Seera Group was selected:**
Seera Group is the premier choice for investment because it represents a direct, scalable, and liquid equity opportunity within the rapidly expanding Saudi tourism and hospitality market. Unlike Riyadh Air, which is a state-owned enterprise, Seera Group is publicly traded on the Tadawul exchange, allowing investors to participate in the company's growth. Seera maintains a dominant market share through its OTA platform, **Almosafer**, and has successfully diversified its revenue streams into high-margin segments like car rentals (Lumi). As Saudi Arabia continues its aggressive tourism expansion under Vision 2030, Seera’s established infrastructure, local expertise, and consistent booking growth make it the most viable commercial vehicle for capturing this sectoral upside.

**Company not selected:** **Riyadh Air**

**Why Riyadh Air was not selected:**
While Riyadh Air is a high-profile, innovative venture and a critical pillar of Saudi Vision 2030, it is not suitable for a standard investment portfolio at this time. Because it is a state-owned, greenfield project (funded by the Public Investment Fund), it is not publicly traded. Consequently, there is no mechanism for private investors to purchase equity in the airline. While it serves as a massive catalyst for the regional travel tech ecosystem—by driving demand and setting new digital standards—it remains a strategic national asset rather than a liquid investment opportunity. It should be monitored for its indirect impact on the broader travel industry, but it cannot be included in a current investment allocation.
